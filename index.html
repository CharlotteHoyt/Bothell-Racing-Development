About Bothell Racing Development


Bothell Racing Development is a student team at the University of Washington Bothell competing in the Formula SAE engineering competition. Formula SAE challenges university students to employ classroom knowledge in an applied setting. By designing, testing, and competing with a small formula-style race car students gain valuable technical skills along with experience in teamwork and project management.
